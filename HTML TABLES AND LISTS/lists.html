Working With Lists

Unordered List

An unordered list starts with the 'ul' tag. Each list item starts with the 'li' tag

  • Chelsea
  • Manchester United
  • Manchester City
  • Leicester City
  • Tottenham Hotspur

Ordered List

An ordered list starts with the 'ol' tag. Each list item starts with the 'li' tag

  1. Chelsea
  2. Manchester United
  3. Manchester City
  4. Leicester City
  5. Tottenham Hotspur

Definition List

A definition list starts with the 'dl' tag.
Each term is defined with the 'dt' tag and the definition is defined with the 'dd' tag

Chelsea
Chelsea Football Club is the best English professional football club.
Manchester United
Manchester United Football Club is a mediorce football club.
Manchester City
Manchester City Football Club is the best professional football club in Manchester.
Leicester City
Leicester City Football Club is an English professional football club based in Leicester.
Tottenham Hotspur
Tottenham Hotspur Football Club is an English professional football club in Tottenham, London, that doesn't competes in the Premier League.

Nested List

A nested list is a list inside another list
